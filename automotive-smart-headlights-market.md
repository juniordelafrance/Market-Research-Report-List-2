<p><strong>The Automotive Smart Headlights Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Adaptive Front-lighting Headlight</li><li>Adaptive Driving Beam Headlight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Smart Headlights market can be categorized into two main types: Adaptive Front-lighting Headlight and Adaptive Driving Beam Headlight. Adaptive Front-lighting Headlights adjust the direction and range of light based on driving speed, steering angle, and road conditions to optimize visibility. Adaptive Driving Beam Headlights use sensors to detect other vehicles on the road and automatically adjust the beam pattern to avoid glaring other drivers while maintaining maximum visibility. These technologies improve safety and convenience for drivers by enhancing visibility in various driving conditions.</p></p>
