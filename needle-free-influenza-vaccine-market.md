<p><p>Needle-free influenza vaccine market includes two main types: Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine and Split Influenza Vaccine. Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine contains a weakened, live virus that can stimulate an immune response without causing illness. Split Influenza Vaccine is made from a virus that has been chemically treated to inactivate it, but still contains proteins that can trigger an immune response. Both types offer a needle-free alternative for administering influenza vaccinations and are increasingly being used to increase vaccination rates.</p></p>
