<p><strong>The Trolling Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bow Mount</li><li>Transom Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Trolling motors are available in two main types: bow mount and transom mount. Bow mount trolling motors are mounted at the front of the boat and offer more precise control and maneuverability. They are ideal for anglers who need to navigate through rough waters or tight spaces. On the other hand, transom mount trolling motors are mounted at the back of the boat and are easier to install and operate. They are a popular choice for smaller boats and anglers who do not require as much control.</p></p>
