AC LVDTs operate using alternating current, providing accurate measurements in dynamic and high-speed applications. DC LVDTs operate using direct current, offering stable and precise measurements in static conditions. Both types of LVDTs are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for various measurement and control applications.</p></p>
