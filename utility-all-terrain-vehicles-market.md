<p><p>Utility All Terrain Vehicles (UTVs) are available in two main types: Crawler Type and Wheeled Type. They are commonly used in extreme off-road conditions where maneuverability is key. Wheeled Type UTVs, on the other hand, feature traditional wheels and are more versatile for general-purpose use.
