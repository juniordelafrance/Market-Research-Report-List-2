<p><p>Zeolite for detergents market can be categorized into various types based on their structure and properties. Zeolite A is commonly used for its high ion exchange capacity and excellent detergency properties. Zeolite P is known for its high physical and chemical stability, making it suitable for harsh detergent formulations. Zeolite X offers superior detergency and anti-redeposition properties. AX zeolite is a combination of A and X types, providing a balance of properties for effective detergent formulations. Each type has unique characteristics that cater to different detergent requirements.</p></p>
