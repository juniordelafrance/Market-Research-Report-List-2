<p><p>DC SPD surge protective devices are essential components in protecting electrical systems from transient voltage surges. There are two main types in the market: solid-state DC SPD and hybrid DC SPD. Solid-state devices offer fast response times and durable performance, making them suitable for high-speed applications. On the other hand, hybrid devices combine the advantages of both solid-state and gas discharge technology, providing enhanced protection and reliability for sensitive electronic equipment. Both types play a critical role in safeguarding DC power systems against damaging power surges.</p></p>
