<p><p>Fire rated ductwork systems can be categorized into two main types: coated and non-coated. Coated fire rated ductwork systems have an additional layer of fire-resistant material applied to the ductwork to enhance its fire protection capabilities. On the other hand, non-coated fire rated ductwork systems do not have this additional layer and rely solely on the inherent fire resistance of the materials used in their construction. Both types of systems are used in various applications where fire protection is a priority.</p></p>
