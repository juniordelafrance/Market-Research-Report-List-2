<p><strong>The Electrocatalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homogeneous Electrocatalysts</li><li>Heterogeneous Electrocatalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrocatalysts are used to facilitate electrochemical reactions in various applications such as fuel cells and electrolyzers. Homogeneous electrocatalysts are soluble in the electrolyte solution, which allows for better control over the reaction. On the other hand, heterogeneous electrocatalysts are solid materials that are typically immobilized on electrodes, providing a larger surface area for the reaction to occur. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and selectivity of electrochemical processes, contributing to advancements in clean energy technologies.</p></p>
