<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Neoprene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Linear Grades</li><li>Precrosslinked Grades</li><li>Sulfur Modified Grades</li><li>Slow Crystallizing Grades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Normal Linear Grades are standard neoprene materials with linear polymer chains, offering good physical properties and chemical resistance. Precrosslinked Grades have increased crosslinking in the polymer structure, providing improved heat and compression set resistance. Sulfur Modified Grades incorporate sulfur into the polymer matrix for enhanced flexibility and toughness. Slow Crystallizing Grades have a slower crystallization rate, resulting in improved processability and uniform properties. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries, providing tailored solutions for different applications.</p></p>
