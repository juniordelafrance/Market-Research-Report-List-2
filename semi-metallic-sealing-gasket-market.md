<p><p>Semi-Metallic Sealing Gaskets come in two main types: Round and Non-circular. Round gaskets are circular in shape and are commonly used for applications where pressure needs to be evenly distributed. Non-circular gaskets are designed in irregular shapes to fit specific sealing requirements, such as flanges with non-standard shapes or sizes. Both types of gaskets are made from a combination of metal and non-metal materials, providing a strong and reliable seal for various industrial applications.</p></p>
