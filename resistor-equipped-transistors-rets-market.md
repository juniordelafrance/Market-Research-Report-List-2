<p><p>Resistor Equipped Transistors (RETs) are transistors that have built-in resistors for convenience in circuit design. They come in three main market types: NPN, NPN/PNP, and PNP. NPN RETs have an N-type semiconductor layer sandwiched between two P-type layers, while PNP RETs have a P-type layer between two N-type layers. NPN/PNP RETs have both NPN and PNP transistors in the same package. Each market type serves specific circuit design needs, offering flexibility and efficiency in various electronic applications.</p></p>
