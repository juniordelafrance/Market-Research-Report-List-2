<p><p>IP Core Chip market consists of two types: Soft Core and Hard Core. Soft Core refers to customizable IP cores that can be modified based on specific requirements of the end-user. They are versatile and can be easily reconfigured for different applications. On the other hand, Hard Core refers to fixed IP cores that are pre-designed and cannot be modified. They are more rigid in nature but offer higher performance and efficiency. Both types cater to different market needs and have their own advantages and disadvantages.</p></p>
