<p><p>The bicycle motors market consists of two main types: hub motors and crank motors. Hub motors are situated in the hub of either the front or rear wheel and provide direct power to propel the bicycle. Crank motors, on the other hand, are mounted near the pedals and work by assisting the rider's pedaling motion. Both hub and crank motors have their own advantages and are popular choices in the electric bicycle market for their efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
