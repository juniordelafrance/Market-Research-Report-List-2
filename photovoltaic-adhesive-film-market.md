<p><p>Photovoltaic adhesive film is used in solar panel manufacturing to bond and encapsulate the solar cells. There are three main market types for photovoltaic adhesive film: transparent EVA film, white EVA film, and POE film. Transparent EVA film allows more sunlight to pass through, maximizing energy production. White EVA film provides better UV protection and reduces heat build-up. POE film is known for its flexibility and durability, making it suitable for various solar panel applications. Each type offers specific benefits and is chosen based on the project requirements.</p></p>
