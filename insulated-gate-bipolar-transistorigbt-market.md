<p><p>Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) market consists of two main types: Modular IGBT and Discrete IGBT. Modular IGBTs are integrated with freewheeling diodes and gate drivers, making them suitable for high power applications. On the other hand, Discrete IGBTs are individual components that are used in low to medium power applications. Both types of IGBTs offer efficient power conversion and are widely used in industries such as automotive, renewable energy, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
