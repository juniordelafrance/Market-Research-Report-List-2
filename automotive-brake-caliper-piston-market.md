<p><strong>The Automotive Brake Caliper Piston Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc Brake Caliper Piston</li><li>Drum Brake Caliper Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Brake Caliper Piston market is divided into two main types - Disc Brake Caliper Piston and Drum Brake Caliper Piston. Disc Brake Caliper Piston is commonly used in modern vehicles due to their efficiency in stopping power and heat dissipation. They work by applying pressure to the brake pads against a rotating disc to slow down or stop the vehicle. On the other hand, Drum Brake Caliper Pistons are used in older vehicles and work by applying pressure to the brake shoes inside a drum to slow down or stop the vehicle.</p></p>
