<p><ul><li>Endothermic</li><li>Exothermic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical foam blowing agents are substances used in the production of foams to create a cellular structure. There are two main types of blowing agents - endothermic and exothermic. </p><p>Endothermic blowing agents absorb heat during the foaming process, reducing the temperature and minimizing energy consumption. On the other hand, exothermic blowing agents release heat during foaming, accelerating the process and enhancing thermal insulation properties. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for producing foams with different characteristics.</p></p>
