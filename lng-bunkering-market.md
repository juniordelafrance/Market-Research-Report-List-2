<p><p>LNG bunkering market involves various types such as truck-to-ship, ship-to-ship, port-to-ship, and others. In the truck-to-ship method, LNG is delivered to the ship by trucks. Ship-to-ship bunkering involves transferring LNG from one vessel to another at sea. Port-to-ship bunkering takes place at designated LNG bunkering terminals in ports. Other methods may include floating bunkering stations or using small-scale LNG carriers. Each type offers unique advantages and challenges in meeting the growing demand for LNG fuel in the shipping industry.</p></p>
