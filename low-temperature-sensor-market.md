<p><ul><li>Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC)</li><li>Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTD)</li><li>Thermocouple Sensor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low temperature sensor market consists of various types such as Negative Temperature Coefficient (NTC) sensors, Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTD) sensors, Thermocouple sensors, and others. NTC sensors provide a decrease in resistance with increasing temperature, RTD sensors use a material with a predictable resistance change over a wide range of temperatures, and Thermocouple sensors measure temperature based on the voltage difference between two different conductors. These sensors play a crucial role in industries such as automotive, healthcare, and electronics for accurate temperature measurements.</p></p>
