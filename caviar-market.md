<p><p>Malossol caviar is a type of caviar that is lightly salted, preserving the delicate flavor of the roe. Pressed caviar is a paste-like product made from leftover caviar eggs that have been pressed together. Salted caviar refers to caviar that has been cured with a high amount of salt to extend its shelf life. Pasteurized caviar undergoes a heat treatment process to kill bacteria and extend its shelf life. These different market types cater to varying consumer preferences for flavor intensity and preservation methods.</p></p>
