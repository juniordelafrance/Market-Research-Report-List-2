<p><p>Low Temperature Fuel Cells (LTFC) are classified into different types based on their operating temperature and electrolyte used. These types include Alkaline Fuel Cells (AFC), Phosphoric Acid Fuel Cells (PAFC), Molten Carbonate Fuel Cells (MCFC), and others. AFCs operate at low temperatures and use an alkaline electrolyte, PAFCs use phosphoric acid as the electrolyte, and MCFCs operate at higher temperatures using a molten carbonate electrolyte. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
