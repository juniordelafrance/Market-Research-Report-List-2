<p><strong>The Metallic Volute Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Volute Pumps</li><li>Multistage Volute Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic volute pumps are classified into two main types: single-stage volute pumps and multistage volute pumps. Single-stage volute pumps have only one impeller and are commonly used for applications requiring low to medium flow rates and pressure. On the other hand, multistage volute pumps have multiple impellers stacked in series to generate higher pressures and are suitable for applications requiring high pressures, such as water supply systems and boiler feed applications.</p></p>
