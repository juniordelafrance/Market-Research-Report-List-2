<p><p>Temperature Compensated Quartz Crystal Oscillators (TCXOs) come in two main market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount TCXOs are typically smaller in size and are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board. Through Hole TCXOs, on the other hand, have leads that are inserted into holes on the PCB for soldering. Each market type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on factors such as space constraints and assembly requirements.</p></p>
