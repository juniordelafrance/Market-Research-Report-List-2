<p><p>Car top tents are available in three main types: soft shell, hard shell, and hybrid shell. Soft shell tents are made of durable fabric and are easy to set up, but may not provide as much insulation. Hard shell tents are made of hard materials like fiberglass or aluminum, offering better protection and insulation. Hybrid shell tents combine the benefits of both types, with a hard top for durability and a soft body for flexibility. Each type has its own advantages and is suitable for different camping needs.</p></p>
