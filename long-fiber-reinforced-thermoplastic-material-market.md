<p><p>Long Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (LFRT) materials are advanced composites made of thermoplastic resins reinforced with long fibers such as glass or carbon. There are two main types in the market: LFT-G (glass fiber reinforced) and LFT-D (carbon fiber reinforced). LFT-G offers a balance of strength, stiffness, and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, LFT-D provides higher strength and stiffness, making it ideal for applications requiring superior mechanical properties such as aerospace and automotive components.</p></p>
