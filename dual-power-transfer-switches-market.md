<p><p>Dual Power Transfer Switches are essential components of backup power systems. There are two main types of Dual Power Transfer Switches - Automatic and Manual. Automatic switches are designed to automatically transfer power sources in the event of a power outage, providing seamless backup power. On the other hand, Manual switches require human intervention to switch between power sources. Both types serve the same purpose of ensuring uninterrupted power supply during emergencies, but Automatic switches offer a hands-free solution while Manual switches require manual operation.</p></p>
