<p><p>Direct Heat Metering System refers to a method of measuring and allocating heating costs in residential or commercial buildings. The Evaporating Style Heat Cost Allocators work by measuring the temperature difference between the radiator and room, while the Electric Heat Cost Allocators utilize electricity consumption data to calculate heating costs.
