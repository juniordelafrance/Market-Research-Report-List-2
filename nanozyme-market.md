<p><p>The Nanozyme market can be classified into four main types: Active Metal Centre Mimic, Functional Mimic, Nanocomposites, and 3D Structural Mimic. Active Metal Centre Mimics are nanoparticles that mimic the catalytic activity of metal ions. Functional Mimics are nanoparticles that mimic the functions of enzymes. Nanocomposites combine different nanoparticles to enhance catalytic activity. 3D Structural Mimics are nanoparticles that mimic the precise three-dimensional structure of enzymes. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries such as healthcare, environmental remediation, and energy production.</p></p>
