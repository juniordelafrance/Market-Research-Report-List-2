<p><p>CCD Cooled Cameras are commonly divided into two market types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. Area Scan Cameras are designed to capture a full image in one snapshot, making them suitable for static scenes or objects. On the other hand, Line Scan Cameras are tailored for capturing moving objects by scanning the image line by line, allowing for clear imaging of fast-moving objects. Both types of cameras utilize CCD cooling technology to reduce noise and improve image quality.</p></p>
