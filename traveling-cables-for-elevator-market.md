<p><p>The traveling cables for elevator market encompasses two main types - flat cables and circular-linked cables. Flat cables are composed of individual insulated conductors laid parallel to each other in a flat configuration, ideal for applications requiring flexibility and space efficiency. On the other hand, circular-linked cables consist of insulated conductors twisted together in a circular formation, providing greater durability and resistance to bending stresses. Both types cater to various elevator systems, offering different advantages based on specific requirements and usage conditions.</p></p>
