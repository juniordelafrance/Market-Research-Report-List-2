<p><p>Coil cleaners are used to clean the coils in HVAC systems to maintain efficiency and performance. There are three main types of coil cleaners: acid-based cleaners, non-acid cleaners, and self-rinsing cleaners. Acid-based cleaners are used to remove tough dirt and grime from coils, non-acid cleaners are safer for use on sensitive materials, and self-rinsing cleaners do not require rinsing after application. Each type of coil cleaner has its own advantages and is suitable for different cleaning needs in the HVAC industry.</p></p>
