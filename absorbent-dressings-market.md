<p><strong>The Absorbent Dressings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Adherent</li><li>Adherent</li><li>Low-Adherent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absorbent dressings are available in different types to cater to specific needs. Non-adherent dressings are suitable for wounds with delicate surrounding skin as they do not stick to the wound bed. Adherent dressings adhere to the wound bed to provide secure coverage. Low-adherent dressings strike a balance between the two, offering gentle adherence without causing trauma during removal. Each type of absorbent dressing serves a unique purpose and ensures effective wound management based on the characteristics of the wound and surrounding tissue.</p></p>
