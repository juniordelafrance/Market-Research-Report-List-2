Another market type is the Gasification process, which converts cellulose into synthesis gas (or syngas) through high-temperature heating in the absence of oxygen. Both of these processes offer sustainable methods for producing ethanol fuel from cellulose biomass sources, contributing to the renewable energy sector and reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
