<p><strong>The Fresh Meat Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP)</li><li>Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP)</li><li>Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) involves modifying the atmosphere inside the packaging to extend the shelf life of fresh meat. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly wraps the meat in a thin layer of film, providing excellent clarity and presentation. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) molds the packaging around the meat using heat and vacuum, offering a customized fit and protection. These packaging types help maintain freshness, prevent contamination, and enhance the overall appeal of fresh meat products.</p></p>
