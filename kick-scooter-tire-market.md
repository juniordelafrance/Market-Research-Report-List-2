<p><p>Kick scooter tire market offers two main types of tires: Solid tires and Pneumatic tires. Solid tires are made of solid rubber and provide a maintenance-free option that is puncture-proof. They are suitable for smooth surfaces and offer a comfortable ride. Pneumatic tires are filled with air and provide better shock absorption, making them ideal for uneven terrain. They require maintenance to prevent punctures and offer a smoother ride compared to solid tires. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the kick scooter market.</p></p>
