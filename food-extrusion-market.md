<p><p>Food extrusion is a process used in the food industry to create a variety of products. Cold extrusion involves pushing ingredients through a die without the use of heat, resulting in products like pasta and cereals. On the other hand, hot extrusion uses heat to soften ingredients before pushing them through a die, creating products like snacks and pet food. Both cold and hot extrusion methods play a crucial role in producing a wide range of food items for consumer consumption.</p></p>
