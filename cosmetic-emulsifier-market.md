<p><p>Cosmetic emulsifiers are substances used in skincare and beauty products to mix oil and water-based ingredients. Natural emulsifiers are derived from plants, fruits, and nuts, and are known for their gentle and nourishing properties. Both types play a crucial role in creating stable and effective cosmetic formulations.</p></p>
