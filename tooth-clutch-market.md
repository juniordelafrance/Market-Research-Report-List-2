<p><p>Tooth clutches come in two main types: Electro Magnetic and Pneumatic. The Electro Magnetic tooth clutches use electromagnetic force to engage and disengage the toothed gears, providing precise and quick operation. On the other hand, Pneumatic tooth clutches rely on air pressure to control the engagement of gears, making them suitable for applications where quick responses are needed. Both types of tooth clutches have their own unique advantages and are used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
