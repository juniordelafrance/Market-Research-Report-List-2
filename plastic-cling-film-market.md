<p><p>Plastic cling film market consists of various types such as PE (Polyethylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride), and PMP (Poly-4-methyl-1-pentene). PE cling film is widely used due to its flexibility and cost-effectiveness. PVC cling film is known for its strong cling properties but may not be suitable for high-temperature applications. PVDC cling film offers excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases. PMP cling film is known for its high clarity and resistance to chemicals, making it ideal for packaging applications.</p></p>
