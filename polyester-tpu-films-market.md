<p><p>Polyester TPU films are available in different types based on their breathability levels. Low Breathable TPU Films are designed to provide minimal breathability, making them suitable for applications where a barrier is needed. Medium Breathable TPU Films offer moderate breathability, balancing barrier properties with some air and moisture permeability. High Breathable TPU Films provide maximum breathability, allowing for air and moisture to pass through easily. Each type caters to specific needs in industries such as medical, food packaging, and textiles.</p></p>
