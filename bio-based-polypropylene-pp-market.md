<p><p>Bio-based Polypropylene (PP) can be produced from different types of biomass sources including sugars, starch, and lignocellulosic biomass. Sugars are simple carbohydrates that can be derived from various plant sources. Starch is a complex carbohydrate found in plants that can be broken down into sugars. Lignocellulosic biomass is made up of cellulose, hemicellulose, and lignin and is commonly found in wood and agricultural residues. Each of these biomass sources can be used as feedstock for the production of bio-based polypropylene.</p></p>
