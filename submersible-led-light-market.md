<p><p>The submersible LED light market comprises stationary type and wall-mounted type options. Stationary type lights are designed to be placed at the bottom of bodies of water, providing a steady source of illumination. They are ideal for creating a calming ambiance or highlighting specific features underwater. On the other hand, wall-mounted type lights are installed on the sides of pools or ponds. These lights are versatile and can be easily adjusted to direct light in different angles, creating various lighting effects.</p></p>
