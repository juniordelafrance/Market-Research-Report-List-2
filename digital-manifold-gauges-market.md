<p><p>Digital manifold gauges are electronic devices used to measure and display pressure readings in refrigeration and air conditioning systems. Two main types of gauges in the market are high pressure gauge and low pressure gauge. High pressure gauges are designed to measure pressures above atmospheric pressure, typically used in the discharge lines of systems. Low pressure gauges, on the other hand, are used to measure pressures below atmospheric pressure, commonly found in the suction lines of systems. Both types of gauges play a crucial role in accurately monitoring and maintaining system performance.</p></p>
