<p><p>Cellulose filaments are categorized into two main types: wood pulp based and refined cotton based. Wood pulp based cellulose filaments are derived from trees and have properties such as high tensile strength and biodegradability, making them suitable for a variety of applications. Refined cotton based cellulose filaments, on the other hand, are derived from cotton fibers and are known for their softness and absorbency, making them ideal for use in products such as textiles and personal care items. Both types of cellulose filaments have unique characteristics that make them valuable in different market applications.</p></p>
