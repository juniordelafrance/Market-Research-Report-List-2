<p><ul><li>Static Line Tensiometers</li><li>Tensiometers for Moving Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elevator cable tensiometers measure tension in elevator cables to ensure safe and efficient operation. Static line tensiometers are used for stationary cables, providing accurate tension readings for maintenance purposes. Tensiometers for moving cables are specifically designed to measure tension in cables that are constantly in motion, such as those used in elevator systems. These devices help to prevent over-tensioning or slackness in cables, which can lead to safety hazards and decreased performance of the elevator system.</p></p>
