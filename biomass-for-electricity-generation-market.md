<p><p>Biomass for electricity generation market includes various types such as boilers, burners, turbines, generators, and others. Boilers are used to heat biomass and produce steam, which drives turbines connected to generators to produce electricity. Burners play a crucial role in converting solid biomass into a gaseous form for combustion. Turbines are used to convert the energy from steam into mechanical energy, which then drives generators to produce electricity. Other technologies in the market include gasification and anaerobic digestion processes.</p></p>
