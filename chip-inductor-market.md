<p><strong>The Chip Inductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Winding Type</li><li>Laminated Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Weaving Type</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip inductors are available in various types based on their construction and materials used. Winding type chip inductors have wire wound around a core. Laminated type chip inductors consist of several layers of conductive material sandwiched between insulating layers. Film type chip inductors have thin film layers deposited on a substrate. Weaving type chip inductors have conductive materials interwoven to form the inductor. Other types include multilayer chip inductors with multiple layers of conductive material. Each type offers unique properties and advantages for different applications.</p></p>
