<p><strong>The 3D Imaging TOF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF (dToF)</li><li>Indirect ToF (iToF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D Imaging TOF Sensor market types include Direct ToF (dToF) and Indirect ToF (iToF). Direct ToF sensors measure the time it takes for light to travel to an object and back, providing accurate depth information for applications like facial recognition and gesture control. Indirect ToF sensors calculate depth by analyzing phase shifts in the reflected light, offering lower accuracy but higher resolution for applications like AR/VR devices and autonomous vehicles. Both types play a crucial role in advancing technology in various industries.</p></p>
