<p><strong>The MPI Copper Clad Laminate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided</li><li>Double-sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MPI Copper Clad Laminate Market offers two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided laminates have a conductive copper layer on only one side of the substrate, making them suitable for simple circuits with components on one side. On the other hand, double-sided laminates have copper layers on both sides, allowing for more complex circuit designs with components on both sides of the substrate. The choice between single-sided and double-sided laminates depends on the specific requirements of the circuit design and application.</p></p>
