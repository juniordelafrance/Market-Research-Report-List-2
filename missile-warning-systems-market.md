<p><p>Missile Warning Systems market includes three main types: Infrared-Based, Pulse Doppler-Based, and Ultraviolet-Based systems. Infrared-Based systems detect heat signatures emitted by missiles, while Pulse Doppler-Based systems use radar technology to track incoming threats. Ultraviolet-Based systems detect ultraviolet emissions from missile exhaust plumes. Each type has its own advantages and capabilities, providing different levels of protection against missile attacks. These systems are vital components of modern defense systems and are constantly evolving to counter emerging threats.</p></p>
