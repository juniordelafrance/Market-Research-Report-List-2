<p><p>Unmanned Automatic Guided Transport Vehicles utilize different types of guidance systems for navigation within industrial settings. Electromagnetic Induction Guidance uses wires embedded in the floor to guide the vehicles. Laser Guidance uses laser beams for precise navigation. Magnetic-gyro Guide relies on magnetic fields and gyroscopes for accurate movement. Each system has its own advantages and is suited for different applications. These technologies enable seamless and efficient transportation of goods within warehouses and factories, improving productivity and reducing costs.</p></p>
