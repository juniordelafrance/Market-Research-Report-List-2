<p><strong>The Coated Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coated Chemical Pulp</li><li>Coated Mechanical Pulp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coated paper market is categorized into two types: coated chemical pulp and coated mechanical pulp. Coated chemical pulp is made using a chemical process where wood fibers are treated with chemicals to remove lignin and other impurities, resulting in a high-quality paper with excellent printability and durability. On the other hand, coated mechanical pulp is produced using a mechanical process where wood fibers are ground and refined to create a paper with good bulk and opacity. Both types of coated paper serve different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
