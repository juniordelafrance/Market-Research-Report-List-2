<p><strong>The Acrylic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Acrylic Resin</li><li>Thermoplastic Acrylic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic resin market can be classified into two main types: thermosetting acrylic resin and thermoplastic acrylic resin. Thermosetting acrylic resin can be permanently hardened by heat or chemical reaction, making them durable and resistant to high temperatures. On the other hand, thermoplastic acrylic resin can be softened by heat and reshaped, providing flexibility and ease of processing. Both types of acrylic resin have their own unique properties and applications in various industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
