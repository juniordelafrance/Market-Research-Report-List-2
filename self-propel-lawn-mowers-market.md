<p><p>Self-propelled lawn mowers can be categorized into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I market includes those mowers that are powered by a gas engine and feature a single-speed drive system, making them suitable for smaller yards with flat terrain. On the other hand, Type II market consists of self-propel mowers with variable-speed drive systems, typically powered by electric motors or lithium-ion batteries. These mowers are ideal for larger yards with sloped or uneven terrain due to their increased maneuverability and control.</p></p>
