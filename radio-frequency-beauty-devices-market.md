<p><p>Radio Frequency Beauty Devices come in different types based on the number of poles they use - Monopolar, Bipolar, and Multiple Polar. Monopolar devices use one electrode to deliver RF energy deep into the skin, while Bipolar devices use two electrodes for more controlled heating. Multiple Polar devices, on the other hand, use three or more electrodes to target specific areas with varying depths and intensities. Each type of device offers unique benefits in terms of penetration depth, skin tightening, and collagen production.</p></p>
