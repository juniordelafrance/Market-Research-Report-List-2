<p><p>POS software for restaurants can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based POS systems store data on remote servers accessed through the internet, offering flexibility and scalability for restaurants of all sizes. On the other hand, on-premises POS systems store data locally on restaurant-owned servers, allowing for greater customization and control over security. Both types of systems have their own unique advantages and drawbacks, catering to the specific needs and preferences of different restaurant businesses.</p></p>
