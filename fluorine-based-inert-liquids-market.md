<p><ul><li>PFAS</li><li>PFPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluorine-based inert liquids can be classified into two main types: per- and poly-fluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) and perfluoropolyether (PFPE) liquids. PFAS are widely used in various industries due to their ability to repel water and oil, as well as their chemical stability. PFPE liquids, on the other hand, are known for their high thermal stability and lubricating properties. Both types of fluorine-based inert liquids play a crucial role in industries such as aerospace, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
