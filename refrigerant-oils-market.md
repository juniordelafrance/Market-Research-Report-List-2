<p><p>Refrigerant oils can be categorized into two main types: mineral oils and synthetic oils. Mineral oils are derived from naturally occurring sources such as crude oil and are commonly used in traditional HVAC systems. On the other hand, synthetic oils are chemically engineered and offer improved performance characteristics such as better lubrication, stability, and compatibility with different refrigerants. Synthetic oils are becoming increasingly popular in modern refrigeration systems due to their superior properties and environmental benefits.</p></p>
