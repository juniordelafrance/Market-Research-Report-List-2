<p><p>Stainless Steel Powder Market Types are categorized based on their microstructure and composition. Martensitic Grade stainless steel powder is known for its high strength, hardness, and wear resistance. Ferritic Grade stainless steel powder offers good corrosion resistance and is magnetic. Austenitic Grade stainless steel powder is non-magnetic and known for its excellent corrosion resistance and high ductility. Each type caters to specific needs and applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
