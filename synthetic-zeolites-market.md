<p><p>Synthetic zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates with a porous structure that make them useful in various industrial applications. Types of synthetic zeolites include Zeolite A, used for detergent production; Zeolite Y, used in catalytic cracking; Zeolite X, used for gas separation; Zeolite ZSM-5, used in petrochemical processes; Zeolite P, used for isomerization reactions; Mordenite, used in adsorption processes; and Zeolite Beta, used in the production of fine chemicals. Each type of synthetic zeolite serves a unique purpose in the market, catering to different industries and applications.</p></p>
