<p><p>Ready-to-eat cereals market can be broadly categorized into two types: pure cereals and composite cereals. Pure cereals consist of only one type of cereal grain, such as corn flakes or rice crisps. On the other hand, composite cereals are a blend of different types of cereal grains, nuts, seeds, and dried fruits. These varieties offer consumers different flavor profiles, textures, and nutritional benefits. Both pure and composite cereals cater to diverse consumer preferences and dietary needs in the breakfast cereal market.</p></p>
