<p><p>Multilayer PET sheet extrusion lines are designed to produce PET sheets with multiple layers, offering better strength, barrier properties, and customization options. They are preferred for applications requiring specific properties in different layers. Single layer PET sheet extrusion lines, on the other hand, produce PET sheets with a single layer, offering simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and ease of operation. Single layer lines are commonly used for applications where basic properties are sufficient and where cost efficiency is a priority.</p></p>
