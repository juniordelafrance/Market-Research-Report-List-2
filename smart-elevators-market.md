<p><p>Smart Elevators can be categorized into different market types based on their operating mechanisms. AC Elevators operate using alternating current, providing efficient and reliable vertical transportation. DC Elevators use direct current for smoother and quieter operation. Hydraulic Elevators rely on a hydraulic system for movement and are best suited for low to mid-rise buildings. Other market types include machine-room-less elevators, geared and gearless elevators, and machine-room equipped elevators, each offering unique features and benefits for different applications.</p></p>
