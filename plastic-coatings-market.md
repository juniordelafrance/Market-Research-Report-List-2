<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Coatings</li><li>Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic coatings market types include solvent-based coatings and powder coatings. Solvent-based coatings are liquid coatings that contain volatile organic compounds and require a solvent to dissolve the coating material before application. Powder coatings, on the other hand, are dry powder coatings that are applied electrostatically and then cured under heat. They are considered more environmentally friendly as they emit little to no volatile organic compounds during the application process. Both types of coatings offer different advantages and are used in various industries for protection and decoration purposes.</p></p>
