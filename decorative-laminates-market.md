<p><p>Decorative laminates can be divided into two main types: high-pressure laminate (HPL) and low-pressure laminate (LPL) markets. HPL is known for its durability and resistance to wear and tear, making it ideal for high-traffic areas. On the other hand, LPL is more budget-friendly and is often used for decorative purposes in residential settings. Both types of laminates come in a variety of colors, patterns, and finishes, providing a versatile and cost-effective option for enhancing the aesthetic appeal of surfaces.</p></p>
