Stainless steel sinks are the most popular choice due to their durability, resistance to corrosion, and easy maintenance. Ceramic sinks offer a traditional aesthetic with a glossy finish but are more prone to chipping and staining. Granite/quartz sinks combine a modern look with the durability of natural stone, making them a stylish and long-lasting option for commercial kitchens.</p></p>
