<p><p>Fish Protein Powder (FPP) market consists of two main types: Fish Protein Concentrate (FPC) and Fish Protein Isolate (FPI). </p><p>Fish Protein Concentrate (FPC) is a type of protein powder made from ground-up fish that has undergone minimal processing, retaining most of the original nutrients. </p><p>On the other hand, Fish Protein Isolate (FPI) is a more refined form of fish protein powder that has been processed to remove more fats and sugars, resulting in a higher protein content.</p><p>Both FPC and FPI cater to different consumer preferences and dietary needs within the fish protein powder market.</p></p>
