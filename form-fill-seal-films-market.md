<p><p>Form Fill Seal Films Market Types refer to the different materials used in packaging for the form fill seal process. Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) films are known for their high clarity and excellent barrier properties. Polystyrene (PS) films are lightweight and offer good moisture resistance. Polyethylene (PE) films are flexible and offer good sealing properties. Polypropylene (PP) films are known for their high heat resistance. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) films are commonly used for shrink wrapping. EVOH films provide excellent oxygen barrier properties. Other materials include Nylon, Aluminum Foil, etc.</p></p>
