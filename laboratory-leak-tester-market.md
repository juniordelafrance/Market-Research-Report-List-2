<p><p>Hammering method and ultrasound are two common types of laboratory leak testers used in various industries. The hammering method uses physical force to create pressure inside a product and detect leaks through sound or pressure changes. On the other hand, the ultrasound market utilizes high frequency sound waves to identify leaks by analyzing the transmission of the waves through the product. Both methods are essential for ensuring the quality and safety of products before they are released into the market.</p></p>
