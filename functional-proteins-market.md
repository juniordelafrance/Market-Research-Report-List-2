<p><p>Functional proteins market can be segmented into three main types: hydrolysates, whey protein concentrates, and whey protein isolates. Hydrolysates are partially digested proteins that are easier to absorb and digest. Whey protein concentrates contain around 70-80% protein and are a cost-effective option. Whey protein isolates are a more purified form of whey protein, containing over 90% protein with minimal fat and lactose. Each type of functional protein offers unique benefits and caters to different nutritional needs.</p></p>
