<p><p>Lightning protecting wires come in two main types- circular section and flat section. Circular section wires are round in shape and provide uniform protection against lightning strikes. They are typically used in areas where a consistent level of protection is needed. On the other hand, flat section wires have a rectangular shape and are ideal for installation on flat surfaces such as rooftops. They offer effective protection while blending seamlessly into the structure they are mounted on.</p></p>
