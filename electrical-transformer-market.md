<p><ul><li>Step Up Transformer & Step Down Transformer</li><li>Three Phase Transformer & Single Phase Transformer</li><li>Two Winding Transformer & Auto Transformer</li><li>Outdoor Transformer & Indoor Transformer</li><li>Oil Cooled & Dry Type Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical transformer market includes various types to meet different voltage requirements. Step Up Transformers increase voltage for long distance transmission, while Step Down Transformers decrease voltage for consumer use. Three Phase Transformers are suitable for industrial applications, while Single Phase Transformers are used in homes. Two Winding Transformers have separate primary and secondary windings, while Auto Transformers have a single winding. Outdoor Transformers are suitable for harsh weather conditions, while Indoor Transformers are designed for indoor installations. Oil Cooled Transformers use oil for cooling, whereas Dry Type Transformers do not require any external cooling medium.</p></p>
