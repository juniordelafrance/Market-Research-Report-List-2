<p><p>Optical rotary encoders are devices used to convert angular position or motion of a shaft into an analog or digital signal. The market for optical rotary encoders can be categorized into two main types: Incremental Encoders and Absolute Encoders. Incremental encoders provide information on the relative position of the shaft, while absolute encoders give the exact position of the shaft at any given time. Both types of encoders are widely used in various industries such as robotics, automation, and automotive for precise motion control and positioning applications.</p></p>
