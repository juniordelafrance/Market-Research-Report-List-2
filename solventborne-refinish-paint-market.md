<p><p>Solventborne refinish paint market consists of clearcoat, basecoat, primer, and sealer segments. Clearcoat is a transparent protective layer applied over basecoat to add gloss and durability. Basecoat provides color and coverage. Primer is used to prepare surfaces for painting by promoting adhesion and corrosion resistance. Sealer is a barrier coat that seals the surface and provides a smooth foundation for topcoats. Each segment plays a unique role in the refinishing process, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
