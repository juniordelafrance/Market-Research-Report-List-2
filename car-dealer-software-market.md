<p><p>Car dealer software can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises market types. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises solutions are installed and maintained on the dealer's own servers, providing greater control and security. Both market types have their own advantages and limitations, with cloud-based solutions being more popular among businesses looking for easy accessibility and quick updates, while on-premises solutions are preferred by those prioritizing data security and customization.</p></p>
