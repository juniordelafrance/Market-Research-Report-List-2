<p><p>Audit Management Systems can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems are hosted on external servers accessed through the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises systems are hosted on local servers within the organization's infrastructure, offering more control and security but requiring a higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of organizations in managing their audit processes effectively.</p></p>
