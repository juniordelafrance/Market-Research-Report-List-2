<p><ul><li>Mono</li><li>Stereo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Camera microphones can be categorized into two main types: mono and stereo. Mono microphones capture sound from a single source, making them ideal for recording a single audio track. On the other hand, stereo microphones have two channels, allowing for a more immersive audio experience with sounds coming from multiple directions. Stereo microphones are popular for capturing ambient sounds and providing a more realistic audio recording. Both mono and stereo microphones cater to different recording needs and preferences in the camera microphone market.</p></p>
