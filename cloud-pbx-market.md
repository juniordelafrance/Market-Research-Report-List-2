<p><p>Cloud PBX services can be categorized into WAN and LAN markets based on their deployment and usage. WAN Cloud PBX solutions are accessed over the internet and offer scalable and flexible communication options for businesses with multiple locations or large remote workforce. On the other hand, LAN Cloud PBX solutions are deployed within a local network and provide cost-effective communication solutions for smaller organizations or single location businesses. Both types offer benefits such as cost savings, advanced features, and improved communication efficiency.</p></p>
