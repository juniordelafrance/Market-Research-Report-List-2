<p><p>Direct restorative materials of dental consumables market can be categorized into types such as amalgam, composite materials, glass ionomers, and other materials. Amalgam, a traditional filling material, is durable and cost-effective. Composite materials, like resin, provide a natural appearance and are versatile. Glass ionomers release fluoride and are commonly used for children or patients with cavities near the gum line. Other materials may include resin-modified glass ionomers or compomers, offering a combination of benefits from both amalgam and composite materials.</p></p>
