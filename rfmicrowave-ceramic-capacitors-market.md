<p><p>RF/Microwave Ceramic Capacitors are used in high-frequency applications due to their ability to withstand high temperatures, have low parasitic losses, and provide stable capacitance values. There are two main types of RF/Microwave Ceramic Capacitors: Class 1 and Class 2. Class 1 Ceramic Capacitors are known for their high stability and low losses, making them ideal for high-frequency and precision applications. On the other hand, Class 2 Ceramic Capacitors have higher capacitance values and are more cost-effective, making them suitable for general-purpose RF applications.</p></p>
