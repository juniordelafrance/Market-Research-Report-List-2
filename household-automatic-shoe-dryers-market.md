<p><strong>The Household Automatic Shoe Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic Shoe Dryer</li><li>Fully Automatic Shoe Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Household automatic shoe dryers can be classified into two main types - semi-automatic shoe dryers and fully automatic shoe dryers. </p><p>Semi-automatic shoe dryers require the user to manually place the shoes on the dryer and set the drying time and temperature. They offer some level of control to the user but still require manual intervention. </p><p>On the other hand, fully automatic shoe dryers operate independently once the shoes are placed inside. They automatically detect the moisture level and adjust the drying time and temperature accordingly, offering a more hands-free drying experience to the user.</p></p>
