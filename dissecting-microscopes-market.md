<p><p>Dissecting microscopes are commonly available in two main types: binocular microscopes and trinocular microscopes. Binocular microscopes have two eyepieces, allowing for comfortable and natural viewing with both eyes. Trinocular microscopes, on the other hand, have an additional third port for attaching a camera or other imaging device, allowing for photography and digital imaging. Both types of dissecting microscopes are widely used in various scientific fields such as biology, veterinary medicine, and materials science for detailed observation and analysis of specimens.</p></p>
