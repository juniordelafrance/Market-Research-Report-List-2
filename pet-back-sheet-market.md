<p><p>PET Back Sheet Market includes two main types: Release PET and High Temperature PET. Release PET is specifically designed to provide a smooth surface for easy release of adhesives, making it ideal for applications in industries like electronics and automotive. On the other hand, High Temperature PET is able to withstand higher temperatures, making it suitable for use in applications where heat resistance is key, such as in the manufacturing of solar panels or other high-temperature environments.</p></p>
