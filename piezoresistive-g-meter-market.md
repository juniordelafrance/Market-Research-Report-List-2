<p><p>Piezoresistive G meter market types refer to the number of axes on which the device can measure acceleration. A 1-axis G meter measures acceleration along a single axis, typically vertical or horizontal. A 2-axis G meter can measure acceleration along two perpendicular axes, providing more comprehensive data. A 3-axis G meter can measure acceleration along all three axes, offering the most complete picture of an object's movement in space. Each type of G meter has its own specific applications and considerations for use.</p></p>
