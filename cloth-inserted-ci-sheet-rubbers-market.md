<p><p>Cloth-Inserted (CI) Sheet Rubbers are available in two main types - Single Layer Cloth and Multilayer Cloth. Single Layer Cloth CI Sheet Rubbers have a single layer of cloth inserted between the layers of rubber, providing additional strength and durability. On the other hand, Multilayer Cloth CI Sheet Rubbers have multiple layers of cloth embedded within the rubber for even higher tensile strength and resistance to abrasion. Both types of CI Sheet Rubbers are widely used in various industrial applications for their superior mechanical properties.</p></p>
