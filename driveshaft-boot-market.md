<p><p>A driveshaft boot, also known as a CV boot, is a protective covering that surrounds the CV (constant velocity) joint on a driveshaft in a vehicle. Driveshaft boots are crucial components in ensuring the smooth operation of the driveshaft and the overall performance of the vehicle.</p><p>The Driveshaft Boot Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period.
