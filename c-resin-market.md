<p><p>C Resin market is classified into various types including C9 Hydrocarbon Resin, C5 Hydrocarbon Resin, C5/C9 Copolymer Resin, Hydrogenated Hydrocarbon Resin, and Others. C9 Hydrocarbon Resin is derived from aromatic cracked petroleum distillates, while C5 Hydrocarbon Resin is obtained from aliphatic cracked petroleum distillates. C5/C9 Copolymer Resin combines properties of both C5 and C9 resins. Hydrogenated Hydrocarbon Resin undergoes a hydrogenation process for enhanced stability. Other types in the market include specialized resins with unique properties for specific applications.</p></p>
