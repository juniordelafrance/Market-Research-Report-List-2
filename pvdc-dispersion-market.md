<p><p>PVDC dispersion is categorized into three types based on its viscosity level: low viscosity, medium viscosity, and high viscosity. Low viscosity PVDC dispersion is ideal for applications requiring good flow properties, such as coating and printing. Medium viscosity PVDC dispersion offers a balance between flow and adhesion properties, making it suitable for a wide range of applications. High viscosity PVDC dispersion is commonly used in applications where a thicker consistency and improved barrier properties are needed, such as in packaging films and laminates.</p></p>
