<p><p>Powdered Metal Gear market is segmented into different types based on their design and application. Spur gears are straight-toothed cylindrical gears used for parallel shafts. Helical gears have angled teeth for smoother and quieter operation. Bevel gears have conical shape teeth for intersecting shafts. Other types include worm gears, rack and pinion gears, and planetary gears which are used in various industrial applications. Each type of gear has unique characteristics and is suited for specific purposes in machinery and automotive industries.</p></p>
