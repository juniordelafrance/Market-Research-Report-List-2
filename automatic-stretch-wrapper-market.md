<p><p>Automatic stretch wrappers are machines used to wrap pallets with stretch film. There are three main types in the market: Rotary Stretch Wrapper, Turntable Stretch Wrapper, and Robotic Stretch Wrapper. </p><p>Rotary Stretch Wrappers use a rotating arm to wrap the pallet. Turntable Stretch Wrappers have a pallet that rotates while the film dispenser moves up and down to wrap the pallet. Robotic Stretch Wrappers use robots to automatically wrap the pallet. </p><p>Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different types of packaging needs.</p></p>
