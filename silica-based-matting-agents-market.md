<p><p>Silica-based matting agents are widely used in various industries for their ability to impart a matte finish to coatings and paints. There are several types of silica-based matting agents available in the market including precipitated silica, fumed silica, and natural silica. Precipitated silica is synthetically produced and offers excellent matting efficiency. Fumed silica is produced through a flame hydrolysis process and has high purity and small particle size. Natural silica is derived from mineral sources. Other types of silica-based matting agents may include modified silicas or silica gels.</p></p>
