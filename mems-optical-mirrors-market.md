<p><strong>The MEMS Optical Mirrors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1-axis Scan Mirror</li><li>2-axis Scan Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>1-axis scan mirrors are MEMS optical mirrors used for single direction scanning applications, such as laser beam steering or barcode scanning. They move along one axis to control the direction of the reflected light. </p><p>2-axis scan mirrors, on the other hand, are designed for dual direction scanning applications, offering more versatility and control over the scanned area. They are commonly used in laser projection systems, 3D scanning, and virtual reality applications. Both types of mirrors play a crucial role in various optical systems and devices.</p></p>
