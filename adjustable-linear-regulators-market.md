<p><p>Adjustable linear regulators are commonly found in two main types of markets, single-channel and multi-channel. In the single-channel market, regulators are designed to provide a single output voltage, making them ideal for applications that only require one regulated voltage level. In contrast, multi-channel regulators are capable of providing multiple output voltages simultaneously, making them suitable for applications that require multiple regulated voltage levels. Both types offer the ability to adjust the output voltage to meet specific requirements, providing flexibility and customization options for a variety of applications.</p></p>
