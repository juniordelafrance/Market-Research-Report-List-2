<p><p>The Sandalwood extract market can be divided into two main types: East Indian Sandalwood Oil and Australian Sandalwood Oil. East Indian Sandalwood Oil is derived from the Santalum album tree, primarily found in India and is known for its rich, woody aroma. Australian Sandalwood Oil, on the other hand, comes from the Santalum spicatum tree, native to Australia, and has a softer, more subtle scent. Both oils are highly sought after in the fragrance and skincare industries for their unique properties and benefits.</p></p>
