<p><p>Dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA) market can be divided into two main types: Axial Bone Densitometers and Peripheral Bone Densitometers. Axial Bone Densitometers are used to measure bone density in the spine and hip, providing critical information about the risk of osteoporosis and potential fractures. Peripheral Bone Densitometers, on the other hand, are more portable devices that can measure bone density in peripheral areas like the wrist, heel, and finger, offering a more convenient option for screening and monitoring bone health.</p></p>
