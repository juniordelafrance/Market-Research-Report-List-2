<p><p>Tagging machines are used to attach tags or labels to products for identification, pricing, or tracking purposes. There are two main types of tagging machines: semi-automatic and fully automatic. </p><p>Semi-automatic tagging machines require some manual intervention from the operator, such as placing the product in the machine or activating the tagging mechanism. These machines are typically used for smaller production volumes or when different tag sizes or placements are needed.</p><p>Fully automatic tagging machines, on the other hand, are completely automated and do not require any manual intervention. They are ideal for high-volume production lines where speed and consistency are essential. These machines are often used in industries such as apparel, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
