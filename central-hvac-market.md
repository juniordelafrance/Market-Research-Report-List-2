<p><strong>The Central HVAC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split System Central HVAC</li><li>Packaged Unit Central HVAC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Central HVAC systems are divided into two main types - Split System Central HVAC and Packaged Unit Central HVAC. Split System HVAC consists of an outdoor unit housing the compressor and condenser, connected to an indoor unit containing the evaporator and blower. This setup is ideal for homes with limited indoor space. On the other hand, Packaged Unit HVAC combines all components into a single outdoor unit, making it suitable for commercial buildings or larger homes with ample outdoor space. Both systems offer efficient heating and cooling solutions for different types of properties.</p></p>
