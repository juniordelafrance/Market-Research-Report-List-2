<p><p>Colored glass absorptive filters are classified into different types based on their transmission characteristics. Longpass filters allow longer wavelengths to pass through, blocking shorter wavelengths. Shortpass filters do the opposite, allowing shorter wavelengths to pass through while blocking longer ones. Other types of filters include bandpass filters that only allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass through and neutral density filters that evenly reduce the intensity of light across all wavelengths. Each type of filter is designed for specific applications and requirements.</p></p>
