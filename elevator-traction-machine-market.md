<p><p>The elevator traction machine market consists of two main types: geared machines and gearless machines. Geared machines use a gearbox to control the movement of the elevator, providing a cost-effective solution for medium-rise buildings. Gearless machines, on the other hand, eliminate the need for a gearbox, offering a smoother and more efficient ride for high-rise buildings. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the elevator industry, catering to different building requirements and preferences.</p></p>
