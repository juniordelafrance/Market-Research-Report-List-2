<p><strong>The Aluminium Die Casting Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminium die casting machinery market consists of two main types: hot chamber machines and cold chamber machines. Hot chamber machines are used for high-speed production of small and medium-sized parts, as the metal is melted within the machine. Cold chamber machines, on the other hand, are suitable for producing larger parts and alloys that require higher melting temperatures, as the metal is melted in a separate furnace before being transferred to the machine. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the aluminium die casting industry.</p></p>
