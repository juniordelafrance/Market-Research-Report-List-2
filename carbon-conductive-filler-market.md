<p><p>Carbon Conductive Fillers are classified into three main types: Elemental Carbon, Carbon Fiber, and Carbon Nanotube markets. Elemental Carbon fillers are made from graphite or carbon black, providing good conductivity at a lower cost. Carbon Fiber fillers offer high strength and stiffness, making them ideal for applications requiring mechanical properties in addition to conductivity. Carbon Nanotubes are the latest advancement, offering superior electrical and thermal conductivity due to their unique structure, making them ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
