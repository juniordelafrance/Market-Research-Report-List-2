<p><p>Heat stable grade lactic acid is available in three main types: L-Lactic Acid, D-Lactic Acid, and DL-Lactic Acid. L-Lactic Acid is the natural form found in the human body and is commonly used in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. D-Lactic Acid is the mirror image of L-Lactic Acid and is often used in the production of biodegradable plastics. DL-Lactic Acid is a racemic mixture of both L and D forms, offering unique properties and applications in various industries such as agriculture, textiles, and healthcare.</p></p>
