<p><p>Vapor deposition is a process used in the manufacturing industry to create thin films of material on a substrate. There are two main types of vapor deposition: chemical vapor deposition (CVD) and physical vapor deposition (PVD). </p><p>CVD involves chemical reactions to deposit a thin film on the substrate, while PVD involves the physical process of vaporizing a solid material and condensing it onto the substrate. Both methods are widely used in industries such as electronics, optics, and coatings to create thin films with specific properties.</p></p>
