<p><p>GaN power modules are classified into two main types: embedded and ordinary. Embedded type modules are integrated directly into the circuit board, saving space and reducing overall system complexity. Ordinary type modules are standalone units that can be easily swapped in and out of a system. Embedded types are ideal for compact designs that require high power density, while ordinary types are more versatile and can be used in a variety of applications. Both types offer high efficiency and reliability, making them popular choices in the power electronics market.</p></p>
