<p><p>Single Phase Transformers are categorized based on their frequency range, including Low Frequency Transformers, Intermediate Frequency Transformers, and High Frequency Transformers. Low Frequency Transformers typically operate below 100 kHz and are commonly used in industrial applications. Intermediate Frequency Transformers operate between 100 kHz and 500 kHz, often found in power electronics. High Frequency Transformers operate above 500 kHz and are prevalent in telecommunications and high-speed applications. Each type of transformer is designed to meet specific performance requirements based on the frequency range they operate in.</p></p>
