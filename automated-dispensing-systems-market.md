<p><p>Automated Dispensing Systems can be categorized into two main types - Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems and De-Centralized Automated Dispensing Systems. Centralized systems are typically large units located in a central pharmacy or storage area, with medications distributed to multiple locations through a network of connected devices. On the other hand, De-Centralized systems are smaller units located closer to the point of care, such as nursing units or operating rooms, allowing for more immediate access to medications and reducing the need for manual processes.</p></p>
