<p><p>Natural hair dye market includes three main types: temporary hair dye, semi- & demi-permanent hair dye, and permanent hair dye. Temporary hair dye lasts for a short period and typically washes out after a few days. Semi- & demi-permanent hair dye lasts longer, usually around 6-8 weeks, and gradually fades away. Permanent hair dye provides long-lasting color that doesn't wash out quickly. Each type offers varying levels of color intensity and longevity, catering to different preferences and needs of consumers.</p></p>
