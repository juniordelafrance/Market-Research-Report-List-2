<p><p>Drum brakes market types include Leading Trailing Shoe Brake, Two Leading Shoe Brake, and Servo Brake. Leading Trailing Shoe Brake utilizes a leading shoe to push against the drum, while Two Leading Shoe Brake has both shoes moving towards the drum. Servo Brake incorporates a hydraulic booster to amplify braking force. These types vary in design and functionality, offering different advantages in terms of braking efficiency and performance in the automotive industry.</p></p>
