<p><p>Contact Bearings are classified into three main types: Ball Contact Bearings, Roller Contact Bearings, and Needle Contact Bearings. </p><p>Ball Contact Bearings use balls to maintain separation between the inner and outer races. Roller Contact Bearings use cylindrical rollers to distribute the load evenly. Needle Contact Bearings have long, thin rollers that offer high load-carrying capacity in limited space. </p><p>Each type of Contact Bearings has its specific design and usage, catering to different applications and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
