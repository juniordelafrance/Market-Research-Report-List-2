<p><p>Intelligent heated toilet seats are available in two main types: tank type and tank-less type. Tank type seats are connected to a water tank that heats up water before supplying it to the seat, providing a consistent level of warmth. On the other hand, tank-less type seats heat water on-demand, resulting in a more energy-efficient and eco-friendly option. Both types offer the benefits of comfort and convenience, with tank-less type being preferred for its efficiency and instant heating capabilities.</p></p>
