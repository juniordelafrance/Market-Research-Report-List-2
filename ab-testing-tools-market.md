<p><p>A/B Testing Tools market consists of two main types: Multivariate and Univariate. Multivariate testing involves testing multiple variables at once to determine the best combination for maximum impact. This approach is more complex but can provide deeper insights into user behavior. On the other hand, Univariate testing focuses on testing one variable at a time to understand its individual impact. While simpler, this method may not capture interactions between different variables as effectively as multivariate testing.</p></p>
