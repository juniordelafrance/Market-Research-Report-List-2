<p><p>Vehicle Hydraulic Retarders are used to slow down the vehicle by providing resistance to its motion. Water Medium Retarders use water as the medium to create resistance, while Oil Medium Retarders use oil for the same purpose. Water Medium Retarders are more common in heavy-duty vehicles, providing efficient braking power. Oil Medium Retarders are known for their stability and reliability, making them popular in commercial vehicles. Both types of retarders play a crucial role in enhancing vehicle safety and control on the road.</p></p>
