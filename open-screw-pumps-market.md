<p><strong>The Open Screw Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Multi-screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open screw pumps are categorized into two main market types: single screw pumps and multi-screw pumps. Single screw pumps consist of a single rotating screw within a cylindrical cavity, while multi-screw pumps have multiple screws working together to move fluids. Single screw pumps are typically used for low to medium flow rates and are known for their simplicity and efficiency. On the other hand, multi-screw pumps are used for higher flow rates and provide increased reliability and stability in pumping operations.</p></p>
