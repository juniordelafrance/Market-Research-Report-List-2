<p><p>Surge Protective Devices (SPDs) are devices designed to protect electronic equipment from power surges, spikes, and other transient disturbances. They work by diverting excess voltage to the ground, preventing any damage to the connected devices. These devices are essential in homes, offices, industrial settings, and commercial establishments to safeguard sensitive electronics such as computers, televisions, and other electrical equipment.</p><p>The Surge Protective Devices Market is witnessing significant growth with a projected CAGR of 10.20% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for SPDs can be attributed to the rising instances of power surges due to factors such as lightning strikes, grid switching, and equipment malfunctions. Additionally, the growth of the electronics and IT industries, along with the increasing awareness about the benefits of surge protection, is driving the market further.</p><p>Latest trends in the Surge Protective Devices Market include the development of advanced SPDs with enhanced features such as remote monitoring, self-diagnostic capabilities, and smart connectivity. Furthermore, initiatives to improve energy efficiency and protect critical infrastructure are likely to fuel the demand for surge protective devices in the coming years.</p></p>

<p><p>Surge Protective Devices Market is segmented into two types: AC Surge Protective Devices and DC Surge Protective Devices. AC Surge Protective Devices are designed to protect against electrical surges in alternating current systems, commonly used in residential and commercial buildings. On the other hand, DC Surge Protective Devices are used to safeguard against surges in direct current systems, commonly found in solar power systems and telecommunications equipment. Both types of surge protective devices play a crucial role in protecting electronic devices and infrastructure from damage caused by power surges.</p></p>
