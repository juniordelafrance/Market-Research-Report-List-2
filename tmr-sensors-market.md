<p><ul><li>Digital Output</li><li>Analog Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TMR sensors market includes two main types - digital output and analog output. Digital output TMR sensors provide a binary output signal, typically in the form of high or low voltage, making them easy to interface with digital systems. On the other hand, analog output TMR sensors provide a continuous output signal that varies based on the measured parameter. Both types of sensors have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on the specific requirements of the system.</p></p>
