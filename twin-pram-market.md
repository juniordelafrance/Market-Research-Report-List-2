<p><p>Front and rear side by side twin prams feature seats that are positioned in a front and rear formation, allowing one child to sit in front of the other. This design provides better visibility for both children and allows for easier maneuverability through narrow spaces. On the other hand, side by side twin prams have seats that are positioned next to each other, allowing both children to interact and see each other easily. This design is often more spacious and comfortable for the children but may be harder to maneuver in tight spaces.</p></p>
