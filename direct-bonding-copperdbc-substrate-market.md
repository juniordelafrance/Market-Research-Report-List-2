<p><p>Direct Bonding Copper (DBC) substrate market includes several types such as Al2O3, AlN, and BeO. Al2O3 DBC substrates are known for their high thermal conductivity and excellent electrical insulation properties. AlN DBC substrates offer even higher thermal conductivity and are often used in high-power applications. BeO DBC substrates are popular for their superior thermal management capabilities, although concerns about the toxicity of beryllium have limited their use. Overall, DBC substrates are integral components in electronic devices requiring efficient heat dissipation solutions.</p></p>
