TDAE is obtained through a specialized distillation process that removes harmful components, making it more environmentally friendly. RAE, on the other hand, is a byproduct of the solvent refining process. Both oils have various applications in industries such as tire manufacturing, rubber processing, and asphalt production. Aside from TDAE and RAE, there are other types of environmentally aromatic oils available in the market with different properties and uses.</p></p>
