<p><strong>The Air Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convoluted Bellows</li><li>Rolling Lobe Bellows</li><li>Sleeve Bellows</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air springs are commonly classified into three types based on their design: convoluted bellows, rolling lobe bellows, and sleeve bellows. Convoluted bellows have a pleated design that allows for greater deflection and stability. Rolling lobe bellows feature a single, continuous chamber that provides a smooth ride quality. Sleeve bellows have a cylindrical shape and are ideal for applications requiring high spring rates. Each type of air spring offers unique benefits and is used in various industries including automotive, transportation, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
