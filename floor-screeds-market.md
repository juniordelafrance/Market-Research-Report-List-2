<p><p>Floor screeds are commonly categorized based on their thickness requirements. Screeds with a thickness of less than 30mm are typically used for thin layer applications, such as smoothing out minor imperfections in existing floors or as a levelling layer before the installation of floor finishes. Screeds with a thickness ranging from 30mm to 60mm are often used for medium-depth applications, such as providing a level surface for tiles or vinyl flooring. Screeds with a thickness above 60mm are primarily used for deep applications, such as creating a strong and level base for heavy-duty flooring materials.</p></p>
