<p><p>Axial carbon seals are used in various industries for sealing applications. The two main types of axial carbon seals are lip seals and combined seals. Lip seals are designed with a flexible lip that provides a barrier to prevent leakage between two surfaces. Combined seals incorporate multiple sealing elements to enhance performance and reliability. Both types of seals are commonly used in rotating equipment to prevent the escape of fluids and ensure efficient operation.</p></p>
