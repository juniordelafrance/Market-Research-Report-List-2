<p><p>Plastics and polymers market is divided into two main types - thermoplastic polymers and thermosetting polymers. Thermoplastic polymers can be melted and reshaped multiple times without undergoing significant chemical changes, making them recyclable and suitable for applications like packaging and consumer goods. On the other hand, thermosetting polymers undergo irreversible chemical reactions when heated and molded, resulting in rigid and durable products used in industries like automotive and construction. Each type offers unique properties and benefits for various applications in the market.</p></p>
