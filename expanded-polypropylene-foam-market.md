<p><p>Expanded Polypropylene Foam is categorized into three main types based on their density and structure: Low Density, High Density, and Porous Polypropylene. </p><p>Low Density Expanded Polypropylene Foam has a lighter weight and softer feel, making it ideal for cushioning and packaging applications. </p><p>High Density Expanded Polypropylene Foam is more rigid and has a higher strength-to-weight ratio, making it suitable for structural applications where durability is key. </p><p>Porous Polypropylene Foam has open pores throughout the material, providing breathability and moisture-wicking properties, making it ideal for applications where air circulation is important.</p></p>
