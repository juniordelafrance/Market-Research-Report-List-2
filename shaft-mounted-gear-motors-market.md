<p><p>Shaft mounted gear motors are commonly classified into two main types based on their orientation: horizontal gear motors and vertical gear motors. Horizontal gear motors are mounted parallel to the ground and are widely used in conveyor systems, cranes, and other horizontal applications. On the other hand, vertical gear motors are mounted perpendicular to the ground and are commonly employed in applications such as elevators, mixers, and other vertical lifting equipment. Each type of gear motor is specifically designed to suit the needs of its respective market.</p></p>
