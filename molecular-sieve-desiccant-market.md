<p><p>Molecular Sieve Desiccant is available in three main types: beads, pellets, and powder. Beads are small, spherical particles that offer high mechanical strength and a large surface area for effective moisture absorption. Pellets are cylindrical pieces that are commonly used in gas and liquid drying applications. Powder is a fine, granular form of the desiccant that is often used for moisture removal in confined spaces. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
