<p><strong>The Sweeper Trucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Broom Sweeper</li><li>Regenerative-air Sweeper</li><li>Vacuum Sweeper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sweeper trucks are categorized into three main types based on the mechanism they use to clean the streets. Mechanical broom sweepers use rotating brushes to sweep debris into a collection hopper. Regenerative-air sweepers use a powerful air flow to lift debris off the ground and into a hopper. Vacuum sweepers utilize suction to collect debris from the surface. Each type of sweeper truck has its own advantages and is suited for different street cleaning requirements.</p></p>
