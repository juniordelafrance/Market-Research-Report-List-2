<p><strong>The Automotive Triaxial Gyroscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interferometric Gyroscope</li><li>Resonant Gyroscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive triaxial gyroscope market consists of two main types, interferometric gyroscope and resonant gyroscope. Interferometric gyroscopes use the interference of light waves to detect motion, offering high accuracy and stability. On the other hand, resonant gyroscopes utilize mechanical resonators to sense rotational movements, providing a cost-effective solution with good performance. Both types play a crucial role in automotive applications, such as vehicle stability control systems, navigation systems, and autonomous driving technologies, contributing to the overall safety and efficiency of vehicles.</p></p>
