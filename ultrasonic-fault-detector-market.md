<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Fault Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw instruments</li><li>Phased Array ultrasonic testing instruments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw instruments are widely used in the market for detecting defects in materials by sending high-frequency sound waves through the material and analyzing the returning echoes. On the other hand, Phased Array ultrasonic testing instruments use multiple transducers to send and receive sound waves at different angles, providing a more detailed and accurate representation of flaws in the material. Both types of ultrasonic fault detectors are essential tools for ensuring the quality and integrity of materials in various industries.</p></p>
