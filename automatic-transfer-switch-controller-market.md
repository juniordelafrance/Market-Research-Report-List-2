<p><strong>The Automatic Transfer Switch Controller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Panel-mounted</li><li>DIN Rail Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Panel-mounted Automatic Transfer Switch Controllers are commonly used in larger industrial applications where the switchgear panel is easily accessible for maintenance and operation. They offer convenient control and monitoring of the transfer switch in one central location. On the other hand, DIN Rail Mounted Automatic Transfer Switch Controllers are more compact and versatile, suitable for smaller residential or commercial applications where space is limited. They can be easily installed on a standard DIN rail inside a control panel, providing efficient and reliable automatic transfer switch operation.</p></p>
