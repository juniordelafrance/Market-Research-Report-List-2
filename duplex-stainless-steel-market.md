<p><p>Duplex stainless steel market consists of three main types - Lean Duplex, Duplex, and Super Duplex. Lean Duplex offers a combination of good corrosion resistance and strength, suitable for applications where cost-effectiveness is key. Duplex stainless steel provides even higher corrosion resistance and strength, making it ideal for more demanding environments. Super Duplex offers the highest levels of corrosion resistance and strength, making it suitable for extremely harsh conditions such as offshore oil and gas platforms. Each type caters to different requirements and industries within the stainless steel market.</p></p>
