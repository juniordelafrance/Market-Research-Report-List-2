<p><strong>The Single Layer Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Layer Capacitor Market is divided into two main types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount capacitors are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a circuit board, making them more compact and suitable for high-density applications. Through Hole capacitors have leads that are inserted through holes in the circuit board and soldered on the other side, offering greater stability and reliability. Both types cater to different needs in the electronics industry, with Surface Mount being more popular in modern compact electronic devices and Through Hole being preferred in applications where reliability is paramount.</p></p>
