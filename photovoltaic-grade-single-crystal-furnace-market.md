<p><p>The Photovoltaic Grade Single Crystal Furnace market includes two main types: Czochralski (CZ) Single Crystal Furnace and Floating Zone (FZ) Single Crystal Furnace. CZ Single Crystal Furnaces are commonly used for producing silicon wafers for solar panels, as they allow for high-quality single crystal growth. On the other hand, FZ Single Crystal Furnaces are preferred for applications requiring ultra-pure and defect-free crystals, such as in the production of high-performance electronic devices. Both types play a crucial role in the photovoltaic industry.</p></p>
