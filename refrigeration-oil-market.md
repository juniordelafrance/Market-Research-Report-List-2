<p><p>Refrigeration oil is divided into two main types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from naturally occurring crude oil and is the traditional choice for refrigeration systems. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is artificially engineered to have specific chemical properties, making it ideal for high-performance applications. While mineral oil is more economical, synthetic oil offers better thermal stability and performance in extreme conditions. Both types have their advantages and are used in various refrigeration systems based on specific requirements.</p></p>
