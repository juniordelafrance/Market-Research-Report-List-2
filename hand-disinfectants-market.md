<p><p>Hand disinfectants market can be categorized into two main types - alcohol-based type and non-alcohol type. Alcohol-based hand disinfectants typically contain ingredients like ethanol or isopropyl alcohol which effectively kill germs and bacteria on hands. On the other hand, non-alcohol hand disinfectants use alternative ingredients such as benzalkonium chloride or triclosan to achieve the same germ-killing effect. Both types of hand disinfectants are widely used in healthcare settings, food service establishments, and households to promote hand hygiene and prevent the spread of infections.</p></p>
