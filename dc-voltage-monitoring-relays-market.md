<p><p>DC Voltage Monitoring Relays are available in two main types: Single-Phase and Three-Phase. Single-Phase relays are designed to monitor the voltage of a single-phase power supply, commonly used in residential and small commercial applications. Three-Phase relays, on the other hand, are suited for monitoring three-phase power systems typically found in industrial and larger commercial settings. These relays provide protection by continuously monitoring the voltage levels and triggering alarms or shutdowns if they fall outside of preset parameters.</p></p>
