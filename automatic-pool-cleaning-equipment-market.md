<p><ul><li>Suction-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Pressure-side Pool Cleaners</li><li>Electric Pool Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic pool cleaning equipment market includes different types of cleaners such as suction-side pool cleaners, pressure-side pool cleaners, and electric pool cleaners. Suction-side pool cleaners work by attaching to the pool's filtration system and moving around the pool to collect debris. Pressure-side pool cleaners use the water pressure from the pool's pump to collect debris and dirt. Electric pool cleaners are powered by electricity and move around the pool on their own, using brushes and suction to clean the pool surface.</p></p>
