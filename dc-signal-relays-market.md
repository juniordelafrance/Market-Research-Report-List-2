<p><strong>The DC Signal Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TH Mounting</li><li>SMT Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>DC Signal Relays Market Types include TH (Through-Hole) Mounting and SMT (Surface-Mount Technology) Mounting. TH Mounting involves the relay pins being inserted through holes in the PCB and soldered on the other side. SMT Mounting, on the other hand, involves the relay being mounted directly onto the surface of the PCB. Both types offer advantages in terms of space-saving, ease of assembly, and reliability. The choice between TH Mounting and SMT Mounting depends on factors such as board design, production process, and application requirements.</p></p>
